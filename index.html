Who I am

§1About

I'm Saket, an Integrated B.Tech + M.Tech CSE student at Central University of Jharkhand, currently in my research year — splitting time between coursework and lab work in deep learning, NLP, and computer vision.

My work centers on turning messy, real-world signals — financial news, cursive handwriting, acoustic stress markers, drug-target molecular data — into models that outperform published baselines. I care about rigorous evaluation: MAE/MAPE over vanity accuracy, cross-dataset generalization (like testing a stress model trained on actors' voices against real NASA Apollo mission audio), and reproducible pipelines over one-off notebooks.

Outside the lab, I run finances for my college's programming club as Treasurer, and spent two years with the NCC building the kind of discipline that carries over into long training runs and longer paper revisions.
